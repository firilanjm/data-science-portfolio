# Social Media Addiction and Student Well-Being

## Research Question
How is social media usage associated with addiction, mental health, sleep, and perceived academic performance among students?

## Data
Survey data from 700+ students including:
- Addiction score
- Mental health score
- Academic performance perception
- Demographic variables

## Methods
- Exploratory data analysis
- OLS regression for mental health outcomes
- Logistic regression for academic impact
- Statistical inference and interpretation

## Key Findings
- Higher addiction scores are strongly associated with lower mental health
- Addiction significantly increases odds of perceived academic impairment
- Demographic effects are weaker once addiction is controlled for

## Tools
Python, pandas, statsmodels, matplotlib
